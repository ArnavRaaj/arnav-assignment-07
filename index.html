Facts About Wakanda

Wakanda has three official languages: Wakandan, Yoruba and Hausa. In the Marvel Cinematic Universe characters from Wakanda are portrayed speaking the South-East African Xhosa language. The Jabari Tribe are portrayed speaking a dialect similar to Igbo from Nigeria.
